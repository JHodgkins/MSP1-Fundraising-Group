Contact Us

If you have any questions, contact us and we will be sure to help.

Fundraising can be a great way to help people and give back to the community where you live.

How to Contact us

Contacting us could not be simplier, use the below form to send us your query.
We only need you to provide a few details.

  • Your name or a nickname, just something we can use to address you in a reply email
  • An email address so we know where to send the reply t your question too.
  • The general subject line of your question, this is only general, you will have a chance to be more specific in the More information section.
  • Your question, we do not mind a long question but please try to keep it to one or two, you can always contact us again - This helps us provide the best answer to the specif questions you have.

We look forward to helping you whatever your enquiry may be.

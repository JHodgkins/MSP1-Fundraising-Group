Fundraise with us

Find out all about how to get started fundraising and available roles within our group.

Every pound raised helps those living with Sight Loss remain independant and gain their mobility back.

Overview

Within our group everyone is a fundraiser, we also have specific roles within our group whose responsability is to fullfill their chosen task, this could be a Coordinator, whose role is to manage and chair the groups meetings and liase with prospectus event holders to secure pitches, to a Treasurer whose role is to manage the takings and ensure all donations are banked efficently and our groups income is calculated correctly every month.

No matter your role, we are all equal and have the same objective in mind, we fundraise to help those living with a visual impairment.

How to Join

Joining our group could not be simplier, select the Apply link below to be taken directly to the Guide Dogs UK website. Select the role you are intrested in, the search will show vacancies within Hertford and Ware, use the search box to enter a new postcode. Then complete the application form, its that simple.
Once you have been signed up, you will be put in touch with our Coordinator who will welcome you and invite you to meet the other group members.

Fundraiser

The Fundraiser role is an all around role where you help out at events and collections, this could mean talking to the public to holding a bucket accepting donations from the public.

Fundraiser role details (opens Guide Dogs UK official site in a new window)

Collection Box Coordinator

The Collection Box Coordinator role entails collecting counter top boxes from businesses within an area and connecting with new businesses and requesting that they hold a box to collect donations.

Occasional Help

Do you only have an hour or two to spare, or looking to fullfill a work volunteering slot?

Occasional help does not require you to be a fully signed up volunteer, but we think you may want too after you help.

Donate

Discover all the ways to support our fundraising goals.

Every pound donated will helps someone living with a visual impairment live their life more independantly.

How you can help?

We have various ways to donate these include:

  • Donate through Just Giving (one off donation)
  • Sponsor a Puppy (monthly donation)
  • Request a Speaker for your school, work or group (voluntary donation)
  • Fundraise with us (no monitry commitment, just your time)

No matter how you choose to support, we appreciate every commitment everyone makes no matter how big or small.

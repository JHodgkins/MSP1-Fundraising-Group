All about our Events & Collections

All our events are scheduled months in advance to give all our volunteers advanced notice and to allow you as a fundraiser to plan the amount of time you can spare to help.
The events we attend are usually fetes, or summer fairs where there will be a good turnout and the public will not mind a charatiable stand sharing their awareness and asking for donations, also these events allow us to have working Guide Dogs and Puppies in training to attend as there is ample space to accomodate.

Our collections usually take place in many of the main high street named supermarkets, we have a good repor with many local branches and the collections can be such a joy for their customers as we are often at the end of the tills or by the entrance welcoming customers and saying farewell, a real treat for the little ones also.
Our collections are normally two to 4 hours in length, so never take up a whole day for our volunteers.
